## Upsell and Cross-Sell AI

Cross-Sell & Up-Sell identifies where existing customers are likely to spend more. By analyzing SKU-level buying patterns across your order history, Endeavor uncovers product pairs, complementary items, and customer accounts with clear expansion potential.

  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem', width: '100%', opacity: 1 }}
  src="/images/product-guide-xsell.png"
  alt="Cross-sell and Upsell Opportunities dashboard - Recommended opportunities and customers to reach out"
/>

Instead of guessing which products to recommend or manually combing through past orders, the system surfaces:

- High-frequency product pairings that naturally go together
- Trigger products that reliably signal a related purchase
- Customers who haven't ordered their usual items recently
- Account-specific product insights to guide targeted outreach

It gives sales and CSR teams a ready-made list of revenue opportunities.
