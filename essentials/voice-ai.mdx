## How does voice to order work?

<Steps>
<Step title="Step 1">
A CSR takes a customer call
</Step>

<Step title="Step 2">
The AI Voice Agent starts listening in real-time
</Step>

<Step title="Step 3">
The customer provides order details
</Step>

<Step title="Step 4">
AI quietly builds the order in the background
</Step>

<Step title="Step 5">
The call is ended and AI completes the entry
</Step>

<Step title="Step 6">
The CSR reviews, edits, and approves the order
</Step>

<Step title="Step 7">
The order is pushed to your ERP system
</Step>
</Steps>
